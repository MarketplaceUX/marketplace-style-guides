App Details → Report Abuse

Provides a way to report inappropriate content or behaviour with the app and submit it to the app developer team.

To access this interface, tap the Report App Abuse button located under the Tools section of the App Details page.

This page contains:

  1. Abuse textbox
  2. Send Report button. This button only appears after a value has been inputted into the abuse textbox.
